Welcome

Greetings from the foothills of West-Central Maine! Whether you are an old friend of East Parish Housing Ministry … a neighbor looking for assistance … or a mission-minded group seeking a new experience sharing God’s love, we hope you find our new website helpful and accessible. Just click on any link above to access a drop-down menu and start your exploration. On these pages you will find useful information regarding EPHM, its history, and its mission; learn about the East Parish communities and the beautiful piece of Maine we call “home”; find out how EPHM works to meet its neighbors at their point of need, what to do if you need assistance, and how you can help; and, just maybe, rekindle old memories and friendships … or start on the path to new ones!
